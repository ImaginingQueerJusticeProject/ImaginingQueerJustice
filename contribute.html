Contribute

This research study is supported by the contributions of community members who want to share their stories and add to the conversation.


Online Contributions

If you would like to write a submission to be used in the study or published to the website, you can do so by completing this form.

Go to Qualtrics Form

This form is for people 18 years and older in NSW who wish to submit a text response to contribute to the Imagining Queer Justice research study.

If you consent for your answers to be included in the study and published on the website, they will be subject to the following conditions:

Identifiable Information

Any information provided in your answer that identifies yourself or another person will be deidentified before publication to the website.

Copyright Infringement

Any content that violates copyright will not be published to the website. Please include attribution and if possible, links to any content drawn from another source or author.

Harmful Speech

Any harmful, violent, or discriminatory speech, will not be published to the website.

Misinformation

Any answers that contain misinformation or are otherwise misleading for readers will not be published to the website.



Qualitative Interviews and Workshops

This research will also be informed by in-depth qualitative interviews and workshops with community members. These research activities will commence in late 2025. if you would like to know more about this, please reach out to the research team via our contact details.
