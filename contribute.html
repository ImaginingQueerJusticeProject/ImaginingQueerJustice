Contribute

This research study is supported by the contributions of community members who want to share their stories and add to the conversation.


The main ways you can contribute are by:

  1. Participating directly via our online questionnaire, the details for this are below.
  2. By sharing this study with your friends, family, community, and network.

Online Contributions

If you would like to write a submission to be used in the study or published to the website, you can do so by completing this form.

Go to Qualtrics Form

This form is for people 18 years and older in NSW who wish to submit a text response to contribute to the Imagining Queer Justice research study.

If you consent for your answers to be included in the study and published on the website, they will be subject to the following conditions:


  1. Identifiable Information: Any information provided in your answer that identifies yourself or another person will be deidentified before publication to the website.

  2. Copyright Infringement: Any content that violates copyright will not be published to the website. Please include attribution and if possible, links to any content drawn from another source or author.

  3. Harmful Speech: Any harmful, violent, or discriminatory speech, will not be published to the website.

  4. Misinformation: Any answers that contain misinformation or are otherwise misleading for readers will not be published to the website.

Share This Study

This research is not looking to promote itself widely on social or traditional media. The best way for it to grow is organically, please feel free to link this website to people in your life who would find it interesting or engaging.

About

Imagining Queer Justice is a PhD research study that seeks to understand how queer people imagine justice, and how it is enacted in the world.
