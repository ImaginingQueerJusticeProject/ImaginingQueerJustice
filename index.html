Welcome to Imagining Queer Justice

This is a PhD research study by Clue Coman, who is a PhD Candidate at UNSW Sydney, supervised by Dr Anthony K J Smith, Prof Christy Newman, and Dr Andy Kaladelfos. This project seeks to understand how queer people imagine justice, and how it is enacted in the world.


The Project

This project is using qualitative methods to look at how justice is imagined, and how these imaginaries of justice interact with our society.


The Community

This research is designed with the intention of involving and giving back to the community. This project is open, communicative, and cooperative. Those that participte can see what others are saying, and see how this project connects to wider conversations and stories of queer justice.


This Website

This website is meant to serve as a centralised point for coordinating with the community members who want to get involved, for sharing resources made to help queer people, and publishing a record of the perspectives and imaginings of all of you.
