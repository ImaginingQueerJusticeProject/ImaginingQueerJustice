The Research Team

Clue Coman PhD Candidate

Clue Coman (they/them) is a PhD candidate and research assistant at the Centre for Social Research in Health, UNSW. Their research explores the social imaginary of queer justice and examines the direct and social harms impacting LGBTQA+ people through the frameworks of critical human rights, queer theory, and zemiology. This thesis interrogates the justice gap between the legal and institutional protections for queer communities, and the reality of their lived experiences. Research Areas: Social imaginaries of justice, Queer theory, Creative research methods.
